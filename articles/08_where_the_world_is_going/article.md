Where the world is going cannot be read from GDP alone. Demography, life expectancy, trade, inflation, debt and regional relationships describe different parts of a country's capacity to thrive.

## A multi-dimensional view

This seven-part analysis begins with demographic divergence and longevity, then follows export growth, macroeconomic health, regional momentum and overlooked economic leaders. The common thread is that national performance is a system, not a ranking.

## Scores are prompts, not verdicts

Composite scoring can reveal patterns that single indicators miss, but it must remain transparent. The reader should see which components drive a result and where data choices affect the conclusion. The purpose is not to name a definitive winner; it is to identify countries and trajectories worth understanding more deeply.
